Following the instructions in the Ultra Square Medium Sized Crosscut Sled video, make a cut on each side of a rectangular piece of wood on your crosscut sled, keeping the wood on the right of the saw blade. Turn the wood 90 degrees counter-clockwise between cuts. After the 4th cut, turn 90 degrees again and cut a narrow strip (around 1/2") off the side of the wood. Measure the far (A) side, the near (B) side, and the length of the offcut.
